Designing With:
A New Educational Module to Integrate Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Data Visualization in Design Curricula.

Dear Receiver Name,

We are thrilled to announce the launch of the Designing With website, an extensive resource to introduce AI in design education and practice, developed by SUPSI in collaboration with EPFL and NOVA. This site showcases the results of the "Designing With: A New Educational Module to Integrate Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Visualization in Design Curricula" research conducted from 2022 to 2024.

The website provides educators and students with a comprehensive set of resources and materials to facilitate and foster their knowledge and integration of artificial intelligence into design practices and teaching activities.

Do you want to learn more about AI for design?

AI for Designers

This section introduces the foundational components and taxonomies developed to integrate AI into the design process. It provides basic AI literacy for students and educators, structuring definitions and examples of 12 AI capabilities, redefining a new general design model, and a new taxonomy of data input and output.

+

Interactive Framework

The interactive framework provides an information environment to guide teachers and design students in choosing the most appropriate Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML) and Data Visualisation (DV) tools to address their design needs. The framework is designed to reduce the complexity of accessing knowledge and to foster the development and practising of a methodology for designing with AI. It is designed to be accessed both on desktop and mobile, to support the exploration and application of AI tools during the design practice through an intuitive interface. The interactive diagram comprises five informative components that streamline the designer's process of researching and selecting AI tools tailored to their practice needs: (a) design stages; (b) AI capabilities; (c) data input; (d) data output; (e) AI tools.

+

Didactic Guidelines

Our didactic guidelines are meant to support teachers in the autonomous implementation of AI courses within design curricula, by providing two main educational modules with detailed educational objectives, teaching activities, and recommendations as examples. This structure ensures the scalability and adaptability of the guidelines across various educational settings.
